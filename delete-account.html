How to Delete Your Account

üìß Option 1: Request Deletion via Email

If you can't access the app, email us with the following information:

Required Information:

  • Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request - Find App"
  • Phone Number: The phone number associated with your account
  • Full Name: As registered in the app
  • User Role: Client or Professional
  • Reason: Brief reason for deletion (optional)

We'll process your request within 3-5 business days and send you a confirmation email.

After Account Deletion

  • You'll receive a confirmation email within 24-48 hours
  • The app will no longer recognize your phone number
  • You can create a new account anytime using the same phone number
  • Previous booking history and ratings will not be restored
  • Outstanding payments or refunds will be processed normally
